# Privacy Policy for Quick UConvert

**Effective date:** July 2, 2026

Quick UConvert ("the App") is a unit conversion app. This policy explains what
data the App collects and how it is used.

## Summary

**The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**
It has no analytics, no advertising, no user accounts, and no network access
of its own.

## Data We Collect

None. Quick UConvert does not collect any personal information, usage data,
device identifiers, or analytics of any kind.

## Local Preferences

The App saves a small set of preferences — your selected theme (light, dark,
or system default) and your unit visibility/ordering choices — using
Android's local `SharedPreferences` storage on your device. This information:

- Never leaves your device.
- Is not transmitted to us or to any third party.
- Is deleted automatically if you uninstall the App.

## Feature Requests and Bug Reports

The Settings screen includes a "Request feature / report issue" button. This
opens an external Google Form in your device's web browser. This step is
optional and only happens if you choose to tap the button. Any information
you submit through that form is subject to Google's privacy policy and is
handled by us only to review feedback and improve the App — not for any other
purpose. We do not automatically send any data to this form; you control
what, if anything, you submit.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including
children under 13, because it does not collect information from anyone at
all.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted at the same
location with a revised effective date.
